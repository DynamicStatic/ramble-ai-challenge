About Ramble

How It Works

1. Daily Challenges

Each day, a new challenge is posted with a unique theme or prompt. You can submit your AI-generated image based on the challenge theme.

2. Creating Images

Use your creativity to write a prompt that will generate an image matching the challenge theme. The more detailed and creative your prompt, the better your results!

3. Community Interaction

Engage with the community by:

  • Liking other users' submissions
  • Leaving comments on images
  • Challenging other users' submissions

4. Challenges and Judging

When you challenge another user's submission:

  • Select one of your own submissions
  • An AI judge will compare both images
  • The winner earns points and improves their standing

5. Leaderboard

Compete with other users on the leaderboard. Points are earned through:

  • Winning challenges
  • Getting likes on your submissions
  • Active participation

Tips for Success

  • Be creative with your prompts
  • Engage with the community
  • Participate in daily challenges
  • Challenge other users strategically
